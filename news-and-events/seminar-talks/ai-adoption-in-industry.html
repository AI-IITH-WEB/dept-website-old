Overview of AI Adoption in Industry - Case Studies and Approach



Seminar talk titled "Overview of AI Adoption in Industry - Case Studies and Approach"

Title Of the Talk: Overview of AI Adoption in Industry - Case Studies and Approach
Speaker: Surendra Tipparaju
Host Faculty: Dr. Maunendra Sankar Desarkar
Date & Time: Saturday, February 17 · 11:00 - 13:00

Abstract:

Artificial intelligence (AI) is transforming various sectors of the economy, from manufacturing to healthcare, by enhancing productivity, efficiency, and innovation. However, adopting AI in industry is not a straightforward process, as it involves various challenges and opportunities. In this session, we will present an overview of the current state of AI adoption in industry. Will also share some best practices and lessons. Finally, we will discuss a approach for AI adoption in industry, covering the key steps and factors to consider. The session is intended for anyone who is interested in learning more about how AI is being used in industry, and what are the main challenges and opportunities. The session will provide insights and recommendations for both AI practitioners and decision-makers, as well as researchers and educators who want to understand the real-world applications and implications of AI. The session will also include a Q&A session, where the attendees can interact with the speakers and ask questions.

Speaker Profile:

Surendra Tipparaju is working as a Principle Architect Manager in Microsoft and He is a Sr. Director in Microsoft from past 16 years. He completed his Masters Degree from Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta.
